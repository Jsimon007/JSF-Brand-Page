Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

John Simon Financial ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website (johnsimonfinancial.com), use our services, or communicate with us, including through our text messaging (SMS/MMS) program.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Financial Information: Income, expenses, and financial goals that you voluntarily share with us during consultations.
  • SMS Consent Data: Your phone number, SMS opt-in consent status, timestamp of consent, the source URL where consent was given, and your IP address at the time of consent.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, and referring sources.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our financial consulting services.
  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations.
  • To send you informational and promotional text messages (SMS/MMS) if you have opted in to our messaging program.
  • To send appointment reminders and service updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.
  • To analyze website usage and improve our user experience.

3. SMS/Text Messaging Privacy

This section specifically addresses how we handle data related to our SMS/MMS text messaging program:

  • No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes.
  • All categories of SMS/text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.
  • We may share your phone number with third-party service providers (subcontractors) who assist us in delivering text messages and providing customer support services, but only to the extent necessary to operate our messaging program.
  • Your SMS opt-in consent and phone number collected for SMS purposes are used exclusively to deliver the messages you consented to receive from John Simon Financial.
  • We maintain records of your SMS consent, including the date, time, phone number, source of consent, and the exact consent language you agreed to, for a minimum of five (5) years.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who help us operate our business (e.g., CRM systems, SMS platforms, web hosting), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to you.
  • Protection of Rights: When necessary to protect our rights, safety, or property, or that of our clients or the public.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption of data in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. SMS consent records are retained for a minimum of five (5) years from the date of consent. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 9).

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access & Correction: You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • SMS Opt-Out: You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. See our SMS Terms & Conditions for details.
  • Email Opt-Out: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link provided in each email.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. We do not use cookies to collect phone numbers or SMS consent data.
